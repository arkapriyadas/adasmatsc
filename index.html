About

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Energy Storage Devices at the Materials Science Centre, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. My research involves the design, optimization, and fabrication of next-generation supercapacitive electrode materials using transition-metal sulfides, composite architectures, and phase-engineered materials.

I have published peer-reviewed research articles in materials science and energy-storage journals and have presented my work at international conferences including ACS, APS, and E-MRS meetings.
