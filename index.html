About

I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Energy Storage Devices at the Materials Science Centre, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. My research background includes the design, optimization, and fabrication of supercapacitive electrode materials, with emphasis on transition-metal sulfides, phase-engineered materials, carbon-based composites, and aqueous supercapacitor devices.

I have published peer-reviewed research articles in materials science and energy-storage journals, presented work at international conferences, and contributed to scientific writing, manuscript handling, and research coordination.
